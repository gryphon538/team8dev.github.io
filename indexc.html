This year's game

In this year's game, Ultimate Ascent, robots attempt to throw frisbees. In the first stage, robotics autonimously try to score goals by shooting frisbees into g\ oals. In the second stage, robot handlers use remotes to shoot more frisbees into the goals. In the final stage, the robots try to climb a ladder / monkeybar type structure. The team is scored by how many goals they make and how high th\ ey climb the structure.
