Summer Camp

Paly Alto High School's robotics team will be hosting a tech camp for middle and elementary schoolers. THe camp will be held in a single two-week session from June 1st - June 14th. From 8:30 am to 5:00 pm, students will learn to build and program small robots and even aquire skills to succeed in 3D animatino. Snacks will be provided but packed lunches are required. We hope to spark interest and start future high schoolers on a path of engineering and technologies.

Dates June 1st - June 14th
Cost $800 for two weeks
