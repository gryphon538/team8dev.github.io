Summer Camp

Palo Alto High Schhool Robotics will be hosting a summer camp for middle schoolers (incoming 6th to incoming 9th) this year. The camp will be held in a single week session from August 3rd to August 7th. Beginning at 9:00 am until 3:00 pm everyday, students will gain experience in the fields of build, programming, CAD/3D Printing and 3D animation. We hope to spark interest and start future high schoolers on a path of engineering and technology. In order to keep costs minimal, we ask for a donation of $200 per camper.

Dates August 3rd to August 7
Recommended donation $200 for one week
