Palo Alto High School Robotics will be hosting a summer camp for middle schoolers (incoming 6th to incoming 9th) this year. The camp will be held in three sessions, one week each. We hope to spark interest and start future high schoolers on a path of engineering and technology. More info on classes will be offered soon.

Dates July 18-22, July 25-29, August 1-5
