Teams of Paly Robotics

Build

The Build team is in charge of building the robot. Build is the largest team.

Programing

The Programing team programs the robot, using C++.

PR

The PR (Public Relations) team informs the public and our sponsors of the team's progress regarding our robot. Many of the pictures throughout this website were taken by the PR team.

Animation

The Animation team makes short stories, representing Team 8 in all FRC animation-related competitions. They are also the smallest team.

Web

The Web team is in charge of creating and maintaining this website and it's content.
