Teams of Paly Robotics

For those who were wondering

Web

Easily the best team, Web is in charge of coding websites, mostly this one. Daily work output comparible to that of a small nuclear power plant, if someone('s chair) hasn't unplugged Rafi's computer. This team is led by Alex "le sweg" Tarng, who uses his head scarf as an eyepatch and seems to think "Dress up as Pirate Day" is every day.

Build

Voted the "coolest" team for the past 7 years running, the Build team is in charge of building...the Robot (Bet you would have never guessed)! They use drills, bandsaws, electric treetaps, 2x4s, glitterglue, fiberglass, iron rods, bolts, tree nuts, duct tape, solenoids, oven mitts, cheese graters, erectronics and empty bottles of unknown alcoholic semiliquids to build a (very sketchy) FRC "approved" robot.

Leadership

The Leadership team meets on Tuesdays during lunch to discuss 'Leadership', a task not easily divined from the team's name. Ironically, the "Communists' Collective Club" also meets Tuesdays during Lunch and, even more ironically, leads better than our Leadership team.

Animation

Voted the second most useful team, behind PR, Animation uses computers to manipulate pictures of blobs on a screen. Apparently, if you get enough of these so-called 'pictures', and flash them fast enough in front of someone's face, it looks like they are moving and telling a story. Most people just get a headache.

Programing

Uses computators to write instructions for the robot, telling it what to to, and whom to chuck frisbees at. Unfortunently, in a miscommunication between the programing and build teams, the robot refuses to interpret any code not written in C.
