Join the Team!

So you want to join the robotics team...

What makes our robotics team different from other teams is that we don't force a class and anybody can join. If you're a Paly student and are interested in making science, by all means join! "Science isn't about why, it's about why not!" -Cave Johnson, Portal 2

Our meetings are always on Thursdays right after school ends, with a little hangout session during advisory for those who don't have it. We are also available on Club Day with our booth on the quad.

Once you join, check with one of the captains or with the mentor to get yourself on the mailing list too. And like our Facebook page! Twitter's cool too. In order to attend any FIRST event, you must also register for a STIMS (Student Team Information Management System) account. Returning members who already made a STIMS account must have their parents check their email for instructions to access the consent form. Questions: See the Contact Us Page .
