Palo Alto High School Vikings

Paly Robotics is dedicated to enriching the education of its members through building, programming, animation, and community outreach. We have been a FIRST team since 1996.




FIRST

The Paly Robotics team is happy to have been a part of FIRST for 18 years. For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology has a term "gracious professionalism" that we believe everyone should abide by. It is the act of sharing with your competitors because you understand that the point of competition is to get better, and that to achieve that goal we need to help each other. To read more about FIRST go to our FIRST page or check out their website.
