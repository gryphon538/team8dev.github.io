Our robot is going to be aimed towards ages 35-55. We are targeting this age group for the following reasons: they have jobs that require long hours, and are also probably raising children. People who work long hours are most likely tired when they come back from work and may simply want to relax before their next work day. The robot is priced at $15,000. We know that the robot is quite expensive, but this piece of machinery is quite worth it. We are expecting 26% of 35-55 year olds to buy our product.
