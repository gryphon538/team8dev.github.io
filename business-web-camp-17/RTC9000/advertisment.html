The RTC 9000

People who lack experience in cooking or have schedules that are too full to allow time for cooking may resort to unhealthy premade packaged foods for themselves and their families. The RTC 9000 is a unique, high-tech robot made for people who want to eat well. Not everyone has the time or experience to compose full, healthy, and balanced meals, so we created the RTC 9000 to help people eat fresh, nutritious food. The RTC is able to cook over 9000 recipes without any human assistance. So sit back, relax, and enjoy!
