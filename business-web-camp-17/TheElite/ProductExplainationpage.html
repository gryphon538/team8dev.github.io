The Elite is a smartwatch and armband designed to push one's limits, improve performance, and make you train harder, longer, and smarter. This innovative product contains two smart wearables which tracks one's activities, then uses that info from the wearer's performance and activities to create graphs and info to upload into the Elite interface. Subsequently, this data is sent to personal trainers, who analyse this, then send feedback to the wearer.

This analysis is designed to drastically improve performance in all aspects, allowing you to better your training and performance. The wearer can then view the feedback from their smartwatch and/or armband. The user can connect to their smartwatch and armband via their cell phones through the Elite app, enabling access 24/7. They can view all feedback, data, information, graphs, and charts, given to them by the Elite system and the trainers. The Elite gives its user choice, letting them control who can access the data.

The smart wearables have been designed by a team of scientists to allow ultimate comfort while you train and perform, enabling you to look, play, and feel your best on and off the field, track pitch, or court. The armband and smartwatch form to fit your wrist and arm shape, then keep your arm cool during training and in performance via the hyperstorm technology, used only by the Elite and its smart wearables. The Elite has been created to function for all sports uses, and its versatility gives its user choice, comfort, and maximised performance. Unlike other smart wearables, the Elite exceeds all expectations by not just giving you data, but sending you precise tips and strategies you can use to improve your game. The Elite has been tested, praised, and approved by young athletes, stating that it drastically improves all aspects of athletics.

The Elite is not just a device, not just a smartwatch or armband, not just a robot, but it is a way of life, and the future of sports around the globe.
