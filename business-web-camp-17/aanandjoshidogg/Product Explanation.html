Our Product, the uPet is a revolutionary product that handles all of the foul and time-consuming work of owning a pet. With the uPet you will never need to worry about forgetting to feed your favorite little buddy, because the uPet will do it for you. The uPet also comes with a built in pooper scooper so it can clean up after your pets.
