The UPet is a product designed to ease your everyday life and improve your relationship with your pet. With the UPet in your home, you will never forget to feed your pet, you will never leave your best friend alone and thirsty all day, and you will never come home to find a smelly "surprise" on you furniture again. The UPet will take away all the monotonous and frustrating aspects of your relationship with your pet, leaving you to enjoy time with your lifelong companion.
